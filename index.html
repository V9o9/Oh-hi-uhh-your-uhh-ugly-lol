About Game

Tiny Fishing is a free fishing game that is relatively easy to play but will help you have moments of extremely comfortable and interesting entertainment

Tiny Fishing Challenge will present various challenges to players who participate in fishing for tiny fish.Players will sit on a small boat and relax with nature and fish. A lot of fish appear, but their small size will challenge the perseverance and ingenuity of each player.

The game is playable on both phones and computers, so you can play it wherever you have access to the internet.

Game information

– Genre: Fishing

– Graphics: 2D

– Game mode: Single player

– Age: 3+

– Publisher: Unity

– Release date: 2022

– Game price: Free

How to play ?

To throw your line out, click with your mouse. Hold the line while circling to capture various fish.

While playing, you can buy additional accessories such as nets, fishing rods, etc. In addition, if you catch a lot of fish at the same time, you will also get a high bonus point compared to catching normal fish. Players will have really quiet moments when participating in the challenge of catching fish in the Tiny Fishing Challenge.

Make money Offline

Making money while you are not online is one of Tiny Fishing’s interesting advantages. This implies that you can take a break from playing when you’re tired and continue to make money. There is no nicer feeling than getting on and finding a lot of money ready for you to spend, therefore you should absolutely use this function.

Check Aquarium

The aquarium in Tiny Fishing is another fantastic resource. The Aquarium is where you save some of your best catches so you may continue to profit from them. Click the aquarium icon in the top right corner, then select the fish to retrieve your money from the aquarium. As you go fishing, be sure to keep an eye out for rare fish that you may put in the aquarium.
